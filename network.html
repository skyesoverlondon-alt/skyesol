How the SOLE Network Operates

Estifarr's Gate Protocol

All gateways operate under the Estifarr's Gate protocol, maintaining integrity, priority access, and secure handshake verification across every entry point in the distributed network.

SOLE Key System

Powered by Firebase authentication, the SOLE key system ensures private portals remain accessible only to verified contract holders and partner organizations.

Public-Facing Arm

The Skye Family Hub represents the public-facing arm — a suite of privacy-first, local-storage family tools available free of charge to all families worldwide.

Distributed Architecture

Each node is purpose-built for its audience: from open-access family tools to key-authenticated restricted zones, all maintained under unified governance and quality standards.
