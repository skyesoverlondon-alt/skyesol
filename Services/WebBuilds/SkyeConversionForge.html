The Problem: Leads Rot in the Gap

Websites are usually built to “look good,” not to behave like a conversion system. If the owner replies late, misses calls, or cannot qualify leads, the site becomes a brochure instead of a machine.

Conversion Forge is designed to close the gap: it captures the lead, confirms next steps, follows up with structure, books the appointment, and records the outcome — with a simple dashboard so nothing gets lost.

The only lead that matters is the one that gets handled.
— Conversion posture

Setup & Install: The Intake Machine

Conversion Forge begins with a one-time install phase. The objective is simple: every serious lead gets captured, routed, followed up, and assigned a status — without relying on “someone remembering.”

  • Lead Capture Architecture

    1 primary intake flow + 2 specialty flows • dynamic questions • spam protections • rate-limit posture.

  • Confirmation + Trust Layer

    Instant confirmation page • next-steps copy • branded SMS/email confirmation scripts • trust + CTA modules integrated sitewide.

  • Booking + Scheduling (Optional)

    Booking page • reminders • reschedule/cancel links • no-show reduction posture.

  • Follow-Up Engine

    7-day sequence • smart branching (book/reply/ghost) • owner notification rules.

  • Lead Vault Dashboard

    Statuses: New / Contacted / Booked / Won / Lost • notes • export to CSV.

  • Conversion Tracking

    Track form submits, bookings, calls (if enabled), deposits • monthly conversion report posture.

Monthly Optimization: Keep the Machine Sharp

After install, Forge can run with a monthly optimization plan to reduce friction, improve follow-up copy, and increase bookings.

Monthly Conversion Tuning

1 form iteration per month • 1 CTA/section test per month • follow-up copy improvements • lightweight A/B posture.

Owner Reporting Loop

One-page monthly conversion summary: what came in, what booked, what closed — and what changed this month.

Forge doesn’t “collect leads.” It turns them into booked decisions.
— Optimization posture
