How It Works

The methodology is simple and brutal: you win by owning multiple authoritative properties that reference your Prime. Search engines understand this pattern because it mirrors reality: real ecosystems link to their main brand.

Executive Authority is designed to look and behave like an authentic network. Each satellite has a defined lane (service lane, location lane, trust lane, category lane, media lane, etc.), publishes consistently, and references the Prime with contextual links—not footer spam.
