FAQ

answers in human terms

Is this just a template?

No. The lane concept is “build once, prove it, deploy many.” Each deployment is branded, configured to the client’s workflow, and launched with real deliverables.

Do clients need to be technical?

No. They use it like a normal system: log in, do the workflow, track status. We provide the human guide and the walkthrough.

How does the 20% + monthly plan work?

Pay 20% to begin. Choose a plan length (6–48 months). The remaining 80% is split evenly across the plan.

How does kAIxU billing work?

The lane is the product. kAIxU is fuel. Clients only pay for AI usage when they use kAIxU features, and usage can be capped so budgets stay clean.

What should a client say to get started?

“We need a system for intake/front office/delivery/client portal. We want it branded as our company. We may want kAIxU Powered later.”
