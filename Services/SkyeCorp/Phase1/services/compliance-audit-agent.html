Overview

What it is: A task agent embedded inside your internal dashboard that runs compliance checklists, gathers proof (screenshots/files/notes), and builds a clear audit trail so you can show “we did it” instead of “trust us.”

Who this is for

If any of this sounds like your day-to-day, this service fits.

  • Busy teamsYou have real work, but it gets stuck in messages and follow-ups.
  • Owner-operated operationsYou’re the glue holding the whole thing together — and you’re tired.
  • Growth pressureMore leads/customers means more tasks, and the system needs a brain.

What you receive

Everything is delivered as a business-ready system you can actually use, not a concept.

  • Built-in task agentThe AI agent lives inside your workflow — not as a separate tool you forget.
  • Clear controlYou decide rules, tone, and approvals. Nothing “mystery happens”.
  • Launch-ready deliverablesYou get a working system plus a simple onboarding runbook.

In plain terms

Businesses that need to stay clean and credible: regulated lanes, teams handling sensitive data, and anyone selling to serious clients who ask hard questions.

Deliverables

This is what you actually receive. It’s long on purpose — you’re buying outcomes, not vibes.

  • Compliance Command Panel (Inside Your App)A simple dashboard: policies, checklists, evidence, and due dates.
  • Monthly/Quarterly ChecklistsClear tasks like “Review access”, “Update policy”, “Confirm backups”, “Confirm billing logs” — written for humans.
  • Evidence Vault WorkflowA place to store proof: notes, files, screenshots, confirmations. Organized by checklist item.
  • Approval StepsFor risky actions (access changes, policy exceptions), it collects context and routes for approval.
  • Audit Trail TimelineA clean timeline: what was done, by who, and when — readable and exportable.
  • Vendor/Client Questionnaire HelperIf a client asks security/compliance questions, kAIxU drafts answers based on your truth.
  • Incident Response Mini-PlanA simple “If something goes wrong, here’s the first 60 minutes” checklist.
  • Training RemindersGentle reminders for basic training tasks (password hygiene, handling sensitive data, etc.).
  • Go-Live Proof RunWe run a full practice audit cycle so the system is proven before you rely on it.

Operational handoff

  • Admin walkthroughA guided walkthrough so you can run it without asking for permission.
  • Usage clarityClear explanation of what triggers AI usage and how caps work.
  • Safety boundariesRules that prevent the agent from promising things you didn’t approve.
  • Launch checklistA simple list to confirm it’s ready: routing, tone, buttons, and escalation.

Onboarding

Simple steps. Human steps. No jargon marathon.

  • Step 1. You tell us what kind of clients you serve and what questions they ask.
  • Step 2. We pick the first set of checklists that matter most to your lane.
  • Step 3. We set who can approve what (owners/managers).
  • Step 4. We install the compliance panel and evidence workflow.
  • Step 5. We run a practice cycle and refine checklists until they’re realistic.
  • Step 6. We launch and set recurring reminders so it stays alive.
