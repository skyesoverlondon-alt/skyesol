Overview

What it is: A support task agent embedded inside your ticket inbox. It drafts replies using your approved knowledge (policies, FAQs), routes tickets to the right person, and keeps customers updated without sounding robotic.

Who this is for

If any of this sounds like your day-to-day, this service fits.

  • Busy teamsYou have real work, but it gets stuck in messages and follow-ups.
  • Owner-operated operationsYou’re the glue holding the whole thing together — and you’re tired.
  • Growth pressureMore leads/customers means more tasks, and the system needs a brain.

What you receive

Everything is delivered as a business-ready system you can actually use, not a concept.

  • Built-in task agentThe AI agent lives inside your workflow — not as a separate tool you forget.
  • Clear controlYou decide rules, tone, and approvals. Nothing “mystery happens”.
  • Launch-ready deliverablesYou get a working system plus a simple onboarding runbook.

In plain terms

Businesses with repeat questions and busy support: SaaS, service businesses, local shops with bookings, and teams handling support via email/DMs/forms.

Deliverables

This is what you actually receive. It’s long on purpose — you’re buying outcomes, not vibes.

  • Ticket Inbox Panel (Inside Your App)A single screen for new tickets, in-progress, waiting-on-customer, and resolved.
  • Instant First Response DraftsA fast, human reply draft that acknowledges the issue and sets next steps.
  • Routing Rules“Billing → finance”, “Schedule → ops”, “Technical → dev”, etc. Simple and visible.
  • Saved Answer LibraryYour approved answers, rewritten clearly. kAIxU uses these first before improvising.
  • Escalation Buttons“Escalate”, “Refund Request”, “Policy Exception”, “Call Back Needed” — each creates a tracked task.
  • Customer Update PingsIf a ticket is waiting, kAIxU prompts a status update so customers don’t feel ignored.
  • Quality GuardrailsRules that prevent making promises you don’t want (refund limits, timeline rules, policy boundaries).
  • Weekly Support InsightsTop issues, repeat questions, and “what we should fix” suggestions.
  • Launch-Day Stress TestWe run a realistic ticket scenario set to ensure tone, routing, and safety are correct.

Operational handoff

  • Admin walkthroughA guided walkthrough so you can run it without asking for permission.
  • Usage clarityClear explanation of what triggers AI usage and how caps work.
  • Safety boundariesRules that prevent the agent from promising things you didn’t approve.
  • Launch checklistA simple list to confirm it’s ready: routing, tone, buttons, and escalation.

Onboarding

Simple steps. Human steps. No jargon marathon.

  • Step 1. You show us your top 20 repeat questions and your policies.
  • Step 2. We convert those into a clean “approved answers” library.
  • Step 3. We define routing and escalation paths (who handles what).
  • Step 4. We install the inbox panel and test with sample tickets.
  • Step 5. We run a live support day test and tune tone + boundaries.
  • Step 6. We launch and monitor for 14 days to lock in consistency.
