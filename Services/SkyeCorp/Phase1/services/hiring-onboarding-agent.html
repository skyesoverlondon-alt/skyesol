Overview

What it is: A task agent embedded inside your hiring pipeline. It screens applicants against your role, drafts interview questions, schedules next steps, and runs a clean onboarding checklist once you hire.

Who this is for

If any of this sounds like your day-to-day, this service fits.

  • Busy teamsYou have real work, but it gets stuck in messages and follow-ups.
  • Owner-operated operationsYou’re the glue holding the whole thing together — and you’re tired.
  • Growth pressureMore leads/customers means more tasks, and the system needs a brain.

What you receive

Everything is delivered as a business-ready system you can actually use, not a concept.

  • Built-in task agentThe AI agent lives inside your workflow — not as a separate tool you forget.
  • Clear controlYou decide rules, tone, and approvals. Nothing “mystery happens”.
  • Launch-ready deliverablesYou get a working system plus a simple onboarding runbook.

In plain terms

Teams hiring contractors or employees who need speed, consistency, and clear steps — without messy inbox chains.

Deliverables

This is what you actually receive. It’s long on purpose — you’re buying outcomes, not vibes.

  • Applicant Pipeline Panel (Inside Your App)New / Screened / Interview / Offer / Hired / Archived with clear next steps.
  • Screening Questions (Human)Short questions that reveal fit fast. kAIxU organizes answers into a clean summary.
  • Interview Pack GeneratorRole-specific interview questions + scorecard in simple language.
  • Scheduling PromptsTemplates to schedule interviews and follow-ups without back-and-forth confusion.
  • Offer + Acceptance ChecklistA step-by-step checklist to send offer, collect acceptance, and confirm start date.
  • Onboarding Checklist (Day 1 / Week 1)Accounts, access, training, expectations, and deliverables — nothing forgotten.
  • Policy Acknowledgement FlowSimple “confirm you read this” steps for your basic policies.
  • New Hire SummaryA one-page summary of the hire: role, pay terms, start date, and first-week tasks.
  • Hiring Activity LogA record of decisions, notes, and who moved the applicant forward.

Operational handoff

  • Admin walkthroughA guided walkthrough so you can run it without asking for permission.
  • Usage clarityClear explanation of what triggers AI usage and how caps work.
  • Safety boundariesRules that prevent the agent from promising things you didn’t approve.
  • Launch checklistA simple list to confirm it’s ready: routing, tone, buttons, and escalation.

Onboarding

Simple steps. Human steps. No jargon marathon.

  • Step 1. You show us one role you hire often (or want to hire next).
  • Step 2. We define what “good fit” means in plain language.
  • Step 3. We build the screening questions and interview pack.
  • Step 4. We install the pipeline panel and test with sample applicants.
  • Step 5. We build your onboarding checklist and first-week tasks.
  • Step 6. We launch and tune after your first real hire goes through it.
