Overview

What it is: A built-in follow-up agent that lives inside your CRM (or lead list). It proposes the next best action for each lead, drafts the message in your tone, and schedules follow-ups — with approvals so you stay in control.

Who this is for

If any of this sounds like your day-to-day, this service fits.

  • Busy teamsYou have real work, but it gets stuck in messages and follow-ups.
  • Owner-operated operationsYou’re the glue holding the whole thing together — and you’re tired.
  • Growth pressureMore leads/customers means more tasks, and the system needs a brain.

What you receive

Everything is delivered as a business-ready system you can actually use, not a concept.

  • Built-in task agentThe AI agent lives inside your workflow — not as a separate tool you forget.
  • Clear controlYou decide rules, tone, and approvals. Nothing “mystery happens”.
  • Launch-ready deliverablesYou get a working system plus a simple onboarding runbook.

In plain terms

Any business that loses money to “I forgot to follow up” — service businesses, agencies, B2B sellers, local shops, and teams with busy phones.

Deliverables

This is what you actually receive. It’s long on purpose — you’re buying outcomes, not vibes.

  • Pipeline Panel (Inside Your App)A clean lead screen: New / Contacted / Proposal Sent / Waiting / Won / Lost, with clear next-step prompts.
  • Next-Step SuggestionsFor each lead, kAIxU recommends the next action (text, call, email, quote, reminder) and why.
  • Message Drafts in Your VoiceShort, respectful drafts that match your style. One-click copy and send.
  • Follow-Up Calendar PromptsSimple reminders: “Send quote”, “Check decision”, “Confirm schedule”. No messy setups.
  • Objection Playbook ButtonsButtons like “Price Concern”, “Need to Think”, “Compare Options” that generate a calm response instantly.
  • Lead Notes CleanupTurns messy notes into a clean summary so anyone can take over the lead.
  • Quote/Offer Attachment FlowA simple way to attach your offer (PDF/link) and track when it was sent.
  • No-Spam GuardrailsRules that prevent annoying messages (frequency limits, quiet hours, opt-out handling).
  • Weekly Pipeline SummaryA weekly report: who needs attention, what’s stalled, and what’s most likely to close next.

Operational handoff

  • Admin walkthroughA guided walkthrough so you can run it without asking for permission.
  • Usage clarityClear explanation of what triggers AI usage and how caps work.
  • Safety boundariesRules that prevent the agent from promising things you didn’t approve.
  • Launch checklistA simple list to confirm it’s ready: routing, tone, buttons, and escalation.

Onboarding

Simple steps. Human steps. No jargon marathon.

  • Step 1. You show us your current lead list and what you sell.
  • Step 2. We map your “normal sales flow” in plain steps.
  • Step 3. We lock your tone (friendly, direct, luxury, etc.).
  • Step 4. We set follow-up timing rules (how often, quiet hours, max touches).
  • Step 5. We install the panel and run a live “lead day” test together.
  • Step 6. We launch and tune for 14 days to maximize closes without spam.
