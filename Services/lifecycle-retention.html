Product Overview
RetainEngine™: Operator-Grade Retention Architecture
RetainEngine™ delivers onboarding sequences, health scoring, churn prediction, win-back campaigns, and loyalty mechanics — all wired into your existing CRM and billing. Built for organizations spending 5x on acquisition and $0 on retention.
💚

Customer Health Scoring

Custom health score model using usage, engagement, support, and payment data. Real-time dashboard with segment views and score-drop alerts.

🔄

Automated Intervention Sequences

Onboarding, nurture, at-risk, win-back, and expansion sequences — triggered by health score changes, usage milestones, and billing events.

Cohort Retention Reporting

Revenue impact reporting, cohort retention analysis, sequence performance dashboards, and monthly retention report templates.

What You Get
RetainEngine™ Deliverables
Every RetainEngine™ engagement includes a comprehensive, hands-on delivery package. Here's exactly what you receive:
🗺️

Lifecycle Mapping

  • Customer journey audit (acquisition → onboarding → active → at-risk → churned → win-back)
  • Touchpoint inventory (every interaction across all channels)
  • Churn signal identification (behavioral + transactional triggers)
  • Lifecycle stage definitions with transition criteria
💚

Health Scoring Engine

  • Custom health score model (usage, engagement, support, payment)
  • Score calculation logic + weighting
  • Real-time health dashboard with segment views
  • Alert triggers (score drops, inactivity, payment failures)
⚡

Automated Intervention Sequences

  • Onboarding sequence (5–7 touch, multi-channel)
  • Engagement nurture (active customer value delivery)
  • At-risk intervention (triggered by health score decline)
  • Win-back campaign (churned customer re-engagement)
  • Expansion / upsell sequences (triggered by usage milestones)
🔗

CRM / Billing Integration

  • Health score sync to CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot, or custom)
  • Billing event triggers (failed payment, downgrade, cancellation)
  • Support ticket correlation (open tickets → health impact)
  • Product usage data pipeline (feature adoption tracking)
📈

Reporting & Optimization

  • Cohort retention analysis (by acquisition source, plan, segment)
  • Revenue impact reporting (saved revenue, expansion revenue)
  • Sequence performance dashboards (open, click, conversion rates)
  • Monthly retention report template
🚀

Launch & Ongoing

  • Retention team training (strategy + tools + playbooks)
  • Retention playbook document (30+ pages, role-specific)
  • 90-day optimization window (test, tune, iterate)
  • Quarterly business review template
