FAQ

answers in human terms

Do my customers have to be technical?

No. The portal is built for normal people. They log in, type, and get answers. If they want deeper controls, you attach Xinth as a managed add-on so they still don’t have to “learn tech.”

Can I sell this under my brand (white-label)?

Yes. The portal is designed to be branded. The kAIxU lane can be positioned as your private AI service, and Xinth can power the governance behind the scenes.

What does “governed access” mean?

Think “digital keycards.” You can decide who gets access, revoke access instantly, and limit usage so costs don’t spiral. You also get logs so you can prove what happened if a client asks.

Is billing included?

Billing is available as an add-on through Xinth. Some customers only need the portal. Customers who want subscriptions, metering, and usage-based billing attach Xinth.

What am I actually buying?

You’re buying a sellable product lane: a member-only AI portal plus (optionally) a real control plane behind it. This is how you turn “AI access” into something that can be sold, governed, and expanded.

Can this be used for internal teams?

Yes. It’s a perfect internal portal: staff log in, use the AI, and usage can be limited per person or per team. It can also be used as a private client support desk.
