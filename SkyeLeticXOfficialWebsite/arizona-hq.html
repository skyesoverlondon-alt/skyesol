Arizona: Where It All Begins

Phoenix is SkyeLetes HQ. Here's why Arizona is the heart of under-6'0" basketball and what makes the desert the perfect proving ground.

Every movement has a birthplace. For SkyeLetes, that place is Phoenix, Arizona—a city built on grit, heat, and the kind of relentless energy that forges champions.

When Skyes Over London LC set out to build a basketball league for players under 6'0", Arizona wasn't just a convenient choice. It was the only choice. The desert demands excellence. The basketball culture runs deep. And the infrastructure was ready.

This is the story of our home state—and why it matters that this is where SkyeLetes was born.

Why Arizona?

Arizona might seem like an unlikely basketball hub. No NBA championships (yet). No dominant college program. But look closer, and you'll see why it's perfect for what we're building:

Year-Round Basketball Weather

While the rest of the country deals with snow and indoor-only seasons, Arizona plays ball 365 days a year. The gyms are always running. The parks are always active. Players here develop faster because they never stop playing.

Hungry Player Base

Arizona players have always been overlooked. Not a traditional recruiting hotbed. Not where scouts come first. But that chip on the shoulder? It produces players who work harder, compete fiercer, and appreciate opportunities when they come.

Growing Population

Phoenix is one of the fastest-growing cities in America. More people means more players. More competition. More talent rising through the ranks. The player pool here is expanding every year.

Strategic Location

As the league expands, Phoenix sits perfectly positioned. Close enough to California, Texas, and Colorado for national events. Major airport. Easy logistics. When state champions converge for nationals, they're coming here.

The Founding Story

SkyeLetes didn't appear out of nowhere. It was built, step by step, from Phoenix:

💡
2024

The Concept

Tyrone Norman III, working from Phoenix, identifies the gap: no premium league exists for under-6'0" players. The vision takes shape.

🔧
2025

Infrastructure Build

Skyes Over London LC begins constructing the systems: registration, media production, uniform design, payout processing.

🚀
2026

Launch Season

SkyeLetes goes live across four states: Arizona, Texas, Colorado, Illinois. Phoenix serves as HQ and national finals host.

National Championship Destination

When state champions from Arizona, Texas, Colorado, and Illinois converge for the national title, they're coming to Phoenix. Here's why that matters:

  • Neutral-ish ground: Arizona champions have slight home advantage, but the venue is designed for all competitors
  • Premium facilities: We're not playing in a community center gym. National finals deserve a national stage
  • Full production: Multiple camera angles. Professional lighting. Broadcast-quality output
  • Event atmosphere: This isn't just a game—it's a destination. Players and families make it a weekend

The Arizona state champion gets to compete in front of home-state fans. The other champions get to prove themselves on the road. Either way, Phoenix delivers the stage these players deserve.

"Coming to Phoenix for nationals felt like a real event. The facilities, the production, the energy—it felt like we'd made it to the biggest stage."
