The Height Check

72 inches. That's the line. Here's exactly how SkyeLetes height verification works and why it protects the integrity of the league.

The entire premise of SkyeLetes depends on one rule being enforced: everyone must be under 6'0" tall. That's 72 inches. Barefoot. No exceptions.

This isn't arbitrary. The height cap is what makes SkyeLetes different from every other basketball league. It's what creates the level playing field. It's what allows skill to matter more than size. And it's why we take verification seriously.

Here's everything you need to know about the height check process.

📏
UNDER 72 INCHES
Maximum height to compete in SkyeLetes
Measured barefoot, standing straight, at official check-in

The Verification Process

Height verification happens at your first official game. Here's the step-by-step:

1

Arrive at Check-In

Get to the venue 30 minutes before your scheduled game time. Check in with staff at the registration table.

2

Remove Your Shoes

Verification is done barefoot. No socks, no insoles, no exceptions. This ensures accurate measurement.

3

Stand Against the Stadiometer

Stand straight with your back against the measuring device. Heels together. Eyes forward. No slouching, no stretching.

4

Measurement Recorded

Staff records your official height. If you're under 72 inches, you're cleared. This measurement stays on file.

5

Get Your Clearance

Once verified, you're marked as height-cleared in the system. You won't need to measure again unless there's a dispute.

"The height check takes 30 seconds. It's quick, it's fair, and it protects everyone in the league."

Why We're Strict

Some people ask: "What's the big deal if someone's 6'0.5"?" Here's why we hold the line:

  • Fairness: The whole point of SkyeLetes is creating a league where height isn't the determining factor. Every inch matters when skills are supposed to be the differentiator.
  • Trust: Players register believing they'll compete against others under 6'0". If we let exceptions slide, we break that trust.
  • Integrity: Championships have cash payouts. The competitive stakes are real. We can't have disputes about whether winners were actually eligible.
  • Precedent: Allow one exception, and the standard erodes. We maintain the line because it matters.

⚠️ What Happens If You're Over 72"

You cannot play. If measurement shows you're 72 inches or taller, you're not eligible for SkyeLetes. Period.

No refunds. Registration fees are non-refundable. You attested to your height when you registered. Misrepresentation is on you.

Immediate removal. If discovered mid-season (through re-measurement due to dispute), you're removed from the roster and all results are reviewed.

Common Questions

What if I'm exactly 72 inches?
72 inches is the maximum. If you measure at exactly 72.0", you're not eligible. The cutoff is "under" 6'0", not "6'0" or under."
What if I measured 5'11.5" at the doctor last year?
Heights vary by time of day, posture, and measurement method. Our official measurement at check-in is what counts. If you're close to the line, get measured accurately before registering.
Can I dispute the measurement?
You can request an immediate re-measure by a different staff member. If both measurements show you over 72", the result stands. We use calibrated equipment and trained staff.
What about hair? I have thick hair.
Measurements are taken with hair compressed. The measuring device presses down to skull level. Hairstyles don't add to your official height.
Can opponents challenge my height?
Yes. If an opposing team formally disputes a player's height, we can re-verify. This rarely happens because we catch issues at initial check-in.
Do I get measured at every game?
No. Once you're verified at your first game, you're cleared for the season unless there's a formal dispute.

Be Honest on Registration

When you register, you attest that you're under 6'0" tall. This is a legal declaration, not a guess. If you're not sure, get measured before you register. Here's how:

  • Doctor's office: Most accurate option. Ask for a height measurement at your next appointment.
  • Home measurement: Stand against a wall, barefoot, with a book flat on your head. Mark the wall. Measure with a tape measure.
  • Gym or fitness center: Many facilities have stadiometers available.

If you're 5'11.75", you might be cutting it close depending on time of day and posture. Know your actual height before you commit.

✓ If You're Clearly Under 6'0"

Don't stress. If you're 5'10" or under, height check is just a quick formality. Walk in, get measured, get cleared, warm up, and play. The process is smooth for the vast majority of players.

The Bigger Picture

Height verification isn't about gatekeeping—it's about protecting what makes SkyeLetes special. Every player who passes the height check knows they're competing on a level playing field. Every game is decided by skill, effort, and teamwork—not by who got the tallest genes.

This is what you signed up for. This is what makes the league unique. And this is why we take 30 seconds at your first game to make sure everyone belongs.

"I've played in leagues where the height rules were 'suggestions.' Here, they mean it. That's why I chose SkyeLetes."
