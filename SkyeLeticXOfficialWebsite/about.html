Our Mission

Building the Premier Stage for Overlooked Talent

Every pickup game has that player—the one who runs the court, sees every angle, and dominates despite being the shortest on the floor. SkyeLetes is for them.

🎯

The Problem We Solve

Traditional basketball systems still miss too many players who can actually hoop. High school seniors and college athletes under 6'0" are often judged by frame before feel, and by height before actual production.

💡

Our Solution

SkyeLeticX now creates a true intake-to-draft league model. Players arrive through the combine, get evaluated in person, and move into a live draft where the 26 founding player-owners build their teams in public.

🏆

Championship Stakes

This is not a random run. SkyeLeticX is being structured as a real league with an executive spine, founding franchises, live draft mechanics, and championship stakes that feel bigger than a weekend event.

📱

Media-First Approach

Every game is filmed in 4K. Every player gets highlight reels. Every team gets content they can use for social media, recruiting, or just showing off. Your game deserves to be seen.

The Journey

How SkyeLetes Came to Be

2024

The Concept

Recognized the gap: talented players under 6'0" needed more than an open gym. Built a league model that could actually evaluate overlooked players instead of talking about them.

2025

Building Infrastructure

Turned Season One into the foundation class. The 26 original players now become the first 26 team owners, giving the league a real internal ownership tier instead of a disposable first season.

2026

Launch & Expansion

Expanded the league architecture into a combine-to-draft model, added a visible leadership chain, and prepared the platform for real expansion after the founding season.
