The Problem: Lyrics Without a Ledger

Every day, songwriters lose ownership of their work—not because someone stole it, but because they never proved they wrote it. In an industry built on collaboration, the question of who wrote what, and when, is almost always answered after the money shows up.

A hook texted at 2 a.m. A verse scribbled on a napkin. A chorus built over a beat in someone else's studio. These moments of creation are the most valuable intellectual property in the music business, yet they exist in the most fragile formats imaginable. Screenshots get deleted. Group chats get buried. Notebooks get lost. And when a song blows up, the only thing that matters is what you can prove.

The existing tools don't help. Copyright registration is slow and expensive. Poor man's copyright is a myth. And split sheets—the industry's standard for dividing ownership—are still handled on paper, in emails, or not at all. By the time a dispute arises, the original moment of creation is long gone.

The Solution: OriginX — Your Lyric Safe & Splitter

OriginX is built around a single conviction: the moment of creation is the most important moment in a song's life. Everything else—the recording, the release, the royalties—flows from that first spark. OriginX captures it, seals it, and gives you proof.

The platform works like a creative safe deposit box. You write your lyrics directly into the vault. The system counts every character, localizes your entry, and seals it with a timestamp. From there, you can split ownership with collaborators, browse your archive, and connect to the broader Sol Enterprises ecosystem through The Hub.

No accounts with major labels required. No expensive legal filings. No waiting. Just write, seal, and own.

Who OriginX Is For

OriginX serves anyone who creates with words and needs to prove it. That means songwriters working solo in their bedroom at midnight. It means producers and topliner teams splitting hooks in real time. It means rappers freestyling verses and wanting a sealed record before they share anything. It means bands who collaborate across cities and need clean ownership documentation before a single note is recorded.

It's also for the growing class of independent artists who don't have a publisher or entertainment lawyer on speed dial. For creators who know their words have value but don't have the infrastructure to protect them. OriginX is that infrastructure—free, fast, and built from the ground up for the independent creative economy.

Why We Built This

The music industry has spent decades building systems that protect labels, publishers, and distributors. The songwriter—the person who actually creates the thing everyone else profits from—has been an afterthought. OriginX flips that.

We built this because we believe every creative act deserves a record. That proof of origin shouldn't cost money or require a lawyer. That split sheets should be digital, instant, and attached to the work itself.
