What It Is

A live showcase + networking experience where Chicago talent can be seen, heard, and supported — without needing permission, connections, or a budget to “prove” they belong.

The Modern Juke Joint Spirit

Inspired by traditional juke joints—places where culture lived loud and unfiltered—this experience creates intentional space for artists to perform, connect, and build with respect. It welcomes singers, rappers, burlesque performers, musicians, and creatives seeking genuine collaboration and visibility in a city rich with talent.

Built for Community + Creative Safety

This is a room engineered for real artistry: clear boundaries, respect-first energy, and community standards that protect the vibe. The goal is simple — build community before artists “blow up,” and strengthen an underground village of real artistry through the mission of #NoGateKeepingChicago.

  • No gatekeeping energy.
  • No pay-to-play pressure.
  • No exploitative promoter culture.
  • Yes to collaboration, integrity, and visibility.

“Music is a healing and connective force — it doesn’t require permission to enter the body or spirit.”

Founded by Chicago artist and creative producer Mahogany “The Mysfit” Mills.

Why It Matters

Chicago artists deserve rooms that prioritize artistry over politics — and people over clout.

Chicago talent is everywhere — access shouldn’t be rare.

Too many artists get discouraged by systems that prioritize clout, budgets, and industry politics over real artistry. Mysfit’s Modern Juke Joint Experience was created as an alternative: a grassroots platform focused on integrity, collaboration, and mental wellness.

Whether you’re polishing your stage presence, looking for creative partners, or simply seeking a healthier scene to build in — this room is designed to meet you where you are and move forward together.

The #NoGateKeepingChicago Promise

We’re building the kind of ecosystem artists can trust. That means:

  • Community over competition — connection is the currency.
  • Authenticity over optics — talent doesn’t need a costume.
  • Creative safety — respect is non-negotiable.
  • Collaboration over extraction — no exploitative energy.
  • Mental wellness matters — art and healing can coexist.

*If you’ve ever felt “not plugged in enough” to be taken seriously — this is for you.
