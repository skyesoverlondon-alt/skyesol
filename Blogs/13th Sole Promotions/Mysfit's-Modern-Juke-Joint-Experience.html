What it is

A live showcase + networking experience inspired by traditional juke joints — built for authenticity, collaboration, and creative safety.

Mysfit’s Modern Juke Joint Experience

This is a live showcase and networking experience rooted in community, authenticity, and creative safety. Inspired by the spirit of traditional juke joints, it creates intentional space for Chicago artists to perform, connect, and build without gatekeeping, pay-to-play practices, or exploitative promoter energy.

  • Perform • connect • build
  • Respect-first energy
  • Community over competition
  • Integrity over industry politics

Why it matters

Chicago artists are often discouraged by systems that prioritize clout, budgets, and politics over real artistry. This event is an alternative: a grassroots platform focused on integrity, collaboration, and mental wellness.

Founded by Chicago artist and creative producer Mahogany “The Mysfit” Mills, the experience is deeply rooted in mental health advocacy — treating music as a healing and connective force.
