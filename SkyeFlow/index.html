    Tutorial

    Follow these steps to driveway automation through SkyeFlow while keeping budgets honest.

    Step 1 · Store the key

    Enter your `KAIXU_VIRTUAL_KEY` in the panel at the top and hit Store key. The key only lives in `localStorage` and gates every gateway call.

    Step 2 · Consult

    Paste the SkyeDocx snippet, describe the governance intent, then click "Consult: Craft plan" to stream a conversational plan and see the delta timeline populate.

    Step 3 · Develop stream

    Switch to Develop mode, press "Develop: Stream deltas", and watch `meta`/`delta`/`done` events update the status pills, ledger, and diagnostics panel.
