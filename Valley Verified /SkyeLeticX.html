📅 The Journey
How SkyeLetes Came to Be
From concept to multi-state championship structure — the evolution of a movement.
2024

The Concept

Recognized the gap: talented players under 6'0" had nowhere to compete at a premium level. Started designing a league that would flip the script on traditional basketball hierarchies.

2025

Building Infrastructure

Developed the Team Owner model, created the Brand-Lock system for uniform customization, and built the player portal infrastructure. Secured partnerships with videographers across target markets.

2026

Launch & Expansion

Launched in Phoenix (HQ), Houston, Denver, and Chicago. First season rosters filling. Multi-state championship structure activated. The movement begins.

Beyond

National Domination

Expansion to 10+ states. National championship tournament. Media partnerships. The under-6'0" league that changed basketball forever.
