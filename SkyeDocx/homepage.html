How it works

The lifecycle is simple: your content is stored locally, and exports are generated on your device. That keeps latency low and privacy high.

1 Create + Write

Open the editor, start a document, and write in a distraction-minimized environment tuned for long-form work.

2 Save locally

Your docs are stored in-browser (IndexedDB). That’s why they open instantly and remain available offline.

3 Export clean artifacts

Choose the output that fits the job: PDF for delivery, TXT for quick transfers, HTML ZIP for archival, or .skye for portable “doc + assets”.

FAQ

Answers for the questions your future customers will ask in their head while pretending they’re “just browsing”.

Does this require an account?

No. The core product is local-first. You can open it, create docs, and export artifacts without creating accounts.

Will it work offline?

Yes, after the first successful load. The service worker caches the app shell (including key CDN assets) so it can launch offline.

Where are documents stored?

In your device’s browser storage (IndexedDB). That’s why it’s fast and private. Exports generate on-device.

Is there a “real” DOCX export?

Current build focuses on PDF/TXT/HTML ZIP and the portable .skye archive format. If you want actual Office DOCX generation, that can be added as a premium module.
