FAQ

Does it truly work offline on first-ever open?
Yes. There are no CDN dependencies. All CSS and scripts are local. The Service Worker caches the shell too. (Remote logo URLs are optional; the default local logo works offline.)
Why upload the logo?
Uploading turns the image into a data URL, so your exported SVG embeds it—meaning the exported files are self-contained and offline-proof.
Does the contact form work offline?
No. Form submits need a network connection. Everything else in the generator works offline.
How do I use my real company logo offline?
Replace assets/logo.svg with your logo (same filename), or upload it in the generator.
