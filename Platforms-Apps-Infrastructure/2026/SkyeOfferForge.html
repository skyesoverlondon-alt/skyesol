The Problem

Pricing delivered in a Word doc is an apology for your rate

The way you present your pricing communicates how seriously you take your own value. A copy-pasted rate table in a Google Doc doesn't say "serious operator." A formal offer letter with deposit schedule, payment terms, and scope breakdown does.

📄

Word Doc "Proposals"

A list of bullet points with a number at the bottom isn't a proposal — it's a quote slip. Clients who receive formal, structured offers close faster and ghost less.

🔢

Manual Pricing Math

Calculating 30% of $18,500 divided across 12 months while trying to write the offer letter is how you send clients wrong numbers. The forge calculates it exactly, every time.

🔄

No Payment Structure

Clients stall on lump-sum pricing. An offer that shows a structureed deposit + installment plan makes the number feel manageable and signals you've done this before.

⏱️

Slow Offer Turnaround

Taking 48 hours to deliver a pricing offer after a discovery call loses deals. SkyeOfferForge lets you forge and send a formal offer within minutes while the conversation is still warm.

Features

Every feature is a deal closer.

Built for operators who send high-value offers and can't afford to look unprepared when the client opens the PDF.

💰

Pricing & Payment Schedule Builder

Enter a total value, choose a deposit percentage (10–50%), and a payment term (6, 12, 18, or 24 months). SkyeOfferForge instantly calculates the exact deposit amount, monthly installment, and total schedule — no spreadsheet required.

📊

Tiered Pricing Engine

Build multi-tier offers with Good / Better / Best pricing structures. Define what's included at each tier, set the price range, and let clients see the value delta between tiers clearly in the offer document — without needing a separate deck.

📋

Formal Offer Letter Format

Every offer outputs as a formal letter — not a quote form. Client name, offer title, effective date, scope summary, pricing table, payment schedule, and a signature block are all structured by default. It reads like an enterprise engagement document.

🤖

AI-Assisted Scope Copy

Provide your service area and bullet-point scope, and the kAIxU layer drafts professional scope language — what's included, what's excluded, deliverables, and assumptions. The kind of scope language that protects you when clients push for "just one more thing."

📄

Branded PDF Export

Export the completed offer as a polished, branded PDF — your logo, your colors, your contact information in the header. The PDF is formatted for print and screen, ready to attach to an email or drop into SkyePortalVault for client access.

✍️

Signature Block Built In

Every offer includes a formatted signature block — client acceptance line, date, and printed name fields. Whether you collect signatures digitally or by PDF, the structure is already in the document without you adding it manually every time.

🧩

Offer Template Library

Save your most common offer structures as templates — your standard brand strategy offer, your retainer template, your project-based pricing structure. Forge a new offer from a saved template in under two minutes for repeat services.

🔗

Direct Portal Delivery

Push a completed offer directly into the client's SkyePortalVault — authenticated, gated, and logged. No email attachment. The client receives a notification, logs into their portal, and downloads the formal offer from their own secure document vault.

📈

Offer History & Version Tracking

Every offer you forge is saved with a version slug. When a client requests changes, you revise and re-export — the history shows the original, the revision, and the final accepted version. No confusion about "which price we agreed on."

How It Works

Configure → Price → Forge → Send

From blank to branded PDF offer letter in four focused steps — no formatting, no math, no templates to maintain in Google Docs.

01
Configure Offer

Name the client, the offer, and the scope summary

Enter the client name, company, offer title (e.g. "Q2 Brand Strategy Build"), and a scope summary. Optionally use the kAIxU AI layer to expand bullet-point scope notes into formal, professionally worded scope language — what's included, what's excluded, deliverable definitions, and standard assumptions.

02
Set Pricing & Terms

Enter total value, deposit %, and payment term

Enter the total engagement value. Select your deposit percentage — SkyeOfferForge instantly calculates the deposit amount and the remaining balance. Choose a payment term (6, 12, 18, or 24 months) and the monthly installment is calculated automatically. If you have multiple tiers, build each tier separately with its own price and scope delta.

03
Preview & Forge

Preview the formatted offer, then generate the PDF

Review the offer in the live preview panel — client name, offer title, scope, pricing table, payment schedule, and signature block are all structured and formatted. Make any last adjustments, then forge the PDF. The branded export is ready in seconds — your logo, your colors, professional layout, print-ready.

04
Send or Deliver

Send the PDF directly or push to SkyePortalVault

Download the PDF and attach it to an email, or push the offer directly into the client's SkyePortalVault for gated, authenticated delivery. Either way, the offer is on the client's screen within minutes of the discovery call ending — while the conversation is still fresh and the momentum is still there.
