The Problem

Operators need a clean financial picture — not a spreadsheet archaeology project

📊

Spreadsheets Break Under Pressure

The moment a spreadsheet becomes your actual financial system of record, it starts accumulating manual errors, broken formulas, and merge conflicts when two people try to update it simultaneously. ExecLedgerPro gives operators a structured ledger built for real use — not formula-watching.

💸

Subscription Accounting Software Is Overkill

QuickBooks, FreshBooks, Wave — they're built for bookkeepers and accountants with features most small operators will never use. ExecLedgerPro is purpose-built for the operator tracking their own numbers — no chart of accounts setup, no double-entry learning curve, no monthly fee.

☁️

Financial Data in the Cloud Is a Risk

Cloud-synced accounting tools mean your revenue numbers, client names, and payment history live on someone else's server. ExecLedgerPro stores all data locally in the browser — encrypted, private, and never transmitted to a third-party server.

📅

Monthly Close Is a Guessing Game

Most solo operators close the month by scrolling through bank statements and trying to remember what every transaction was. ExecLedgerPro tracks entries as they happen with category tags — so monthly close is a review, not a reconstruction project.

Features

A clean financial ledger built for the operator who tracks their own numbers.

📥

Income & Expense Logging

Log every revenue and expense entry with description, amount, category tag, and date. Built-in category system covers the most common operator transaction types — Revenue, Contractor, Operations, Marketing, Software, and custom categories you define.

📈

Live P&L View

Total revenue minus total expenses, updated instantly as you log entries. The P&L view breaks down by month, quarter, and year-to-date — so you always know where you stand without running a report or asking your accountant.

💧

Cash Flow Runway Calculator

ExecLedgerPro calculates your average monthly burn rate from your expense history and divides your current cash balance by it — giving you a real-time runway figure in months. Know exactly when you need to either close more revenue or cut costs.

📋

Monthly Close Report

One-click monthly summary: total revenue, total expenses, net P&L, top expense categories, and year-over-year comparison if prior year data is present. Export as a PDF or CSV for your accountant or for your own records without reformatting anything.

🏷️

Category Tagging & Filters

Every entry gets a category tag. Filter your ledger by category to see exactly how much you spend on contractors, software, or any custom category you've defined. Spot your largest expense categories at a glance — no pivot table required.

📶

Offline-First PWA

ExecLedgerPro runs fully offline after the first load. Install it as a PWA and log entries in a remote client office, on a plane, or anywhere without internet. All data is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB — private, encrypted, never transmitted.

🔄

Recurring Entry Templates

Set up recurring entries for predictable income (monthly retainers) or fixed expenses (software subscriptions, rent). ExecLedgerPro auto-populates these entries each period so your ledger stays accurate without manual re-entry every month.

💾

Local Backup & Restore

Export your entire ledger as an encrypted JSON backup file at any time and restore it to any device. No cloud account required — your financial history travels with you on your own terms, in your own hands, on your own schedule.

How It Works

Install → Log → Review → Close

01
Install

Open ExecLedgerPro and install it as a PWA — offline and ready in under a minute

Visit ExecLedgerPro in your browser and install it from the PWA prompt. All app data is cached immediately — from that point on it runs fully offline. No account creation, no email verification, no cloud setup required before you can log your first entry.

02
Log

Enter income and expense entries as they happen — takes under 20 seconds per entry

Tap "+ New Entry," select Income or Expense, enter the amount, description, and category, and tap Save. The entry appears in your ledger immediately and the P&L view updates in real time. Log entries daily, weekly, or as transactions happen — whichever fits your workflow.

03
Review

Check your P&L, cash flow runway, and category breakdown any time

Open the Dashboard to see your live P&L, runway calculation, and top expense categories at a glance. Drill into any category to see every associated entry. Filter by month or quarter to compare performance over time — no report generation required.

04
Close

Run the monthly close — export your summary for your records or your accountant

On the last day of the month, tap "Monthly Close" to generate a clean revenue, expense, and P&L summary for the month. Export it as PDF or CSV. Forward it to your accountant if you use one — they get clean numbers without needing access to your system.
