The Problem

Operators with 10+ tools spend 20 minutes a day finding the right one to open

📑

Bookmark Folders Don't Scale

Browser bookmark folders work fine for 10 links. By 50 deployed tools across multiple clients, environments, and systems, bookmark folders become a nested hierarchy no one can navigate under pressure. GraySole gives each tool a named, searchable tile you can see at a glance.

🟡

No Way to Know If an App Is Down

Without GraySole's status ping, you click a bookmark, wait for the page to load, and only then discover the app is returning a 503. GraySole pings every registered app on load and shows green/yellow/red status so you know before you click what's actually running.

📱

Your Tools Aren't on Your Home Screen

Your deployed apps live in some bookmarks folder on desktop but you switch to mobile constantly. GraySole installs as a PWA on every device — one hub on your phone home screen that has every tool you operate, accessible anywhere, even when some of those tools are offline.

🤝

Client Handoffs Are Confusing

When you hand off a set of tools to a client or team member, sending a list of URLs in an email is the usual approach. GraySole's shareable hub lets you publish a branded launcher page that gives them a single organized URL for every tool they have access to.

Features

One hub. Every tool. Always one click away.

🚀

Quick-Launch Tiles

Every registered app or URL becomes a named tile with a custom icon, category tag, and one-click launch. Tiles show status at a glance and open the target URL in one tap — no bookmark hunting, no address bar typing.

🔴

Live Status Pings

GraySole pings each registered URL when you open the launcher and displays a live green/yellow/red status indicator on every tile. Know which apps are up before you click — save troubleshooting time by seeing outages immediately.

🗂️

Category Groups

Organize your launcher tiles into named category groups — Finance & Ledger, Client Portals, Dev Tools, Admin Systems, or any grouping that matches your operation. Collapse and expand groups to keep your hub uncluttered as it grows.

⌨️

Keyboard Quick-Launch

Hit a configurable keyboard shortcut to open the GraySole search bar from anywhere. Type the first few characters of any app name and press Enter to launch it — never reach for the mouse or touch the bookmarks bar again.

📱

Offline-First PWA

Install GraySole on your phone, tablet, and desktop — it runs offline-first with the full launcher UI cached locally. Even when individual apps are down or you're on a plane, your launcher and app catalog are available for reference.

🌐

Shareable Team Hub

Publish your GraySole hub as a branded page and share a single URL with your team or clients. They get an organized, searchable launcher for every tool they have access to — no more "where do I find X" questions in Slack.

📝

App Notes & Credentials Hints

Each launcher tile can store a private notes field — login hints, environment notes (dev vs prod), or quick reference info. Notes are stored locally and never transmitted. A quick tap on the info icon shows the notes without opening the app.

⚖️

Environment Tagging

Tag each app tile as Production, Staging, Dev, or Client — and filter the launcher to show only the environment you're working in. Eliminate the risk of hitting production when you meant staging by clearly separating environment tiles.

How It Works

Install → Register → Organize → Launch

01
Install

Open GraySole Launcher and install it as a PWA on every device you use

Visit GraySole Launcher in your browser and install from the PWA prompt. Install on your phone, tablet, and desktop — your app catalog syncs across devices via the optional JSON backup system so every device sees the same launcher.

02
Register

Add each app you operate as a named tile with icon, category, and optional notes

Tap "+ Add App," enter the URL, give it a name and icon emoji, assign a category group, add environment tag (Prod/Staging/Dev), and write an optional private notes field. Takes 30 seconds per app. Add all your current tools in one session.

03
Organize

Arrange tiles into category groups — customize your hub layout to match your workflow

Drag tiles into position within each category group. Rename or reorder groups. Pin your most-used tiles to a top-row "Favorites" section for instant access. The layout you create is saved locally and persists across sessions.

04
Launch

Open GraySole each day — see status, search, and launch any tool in one click

GraySole becomes your daily starting point. Open it, see which apps are green, use keyboard search to jump to any tool, and launch in one tap. No more bookmark hunting, no more pasting URLs, no more wondering which environment you're in.
