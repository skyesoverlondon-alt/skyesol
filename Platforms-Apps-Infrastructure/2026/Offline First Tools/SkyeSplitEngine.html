The Problem

Revenue splits calculated on a napkin create disputes, errors, and distrust on the team

When payment splits are ad-hoc, undocumented, and calculated differently each time, someone always feels shorted — and the argument always happens after the job is done.

🧮

Mental Math Mistakes

Splitting revenue mentally — or on a basic calculator — introduces rounding errors, forgotten deductions, and inconsistent treatment of expenses. SkyeSplitEngine calculates every split precisely, applies deductions in the correct order, and shows the full breakdown so everyone sees exactly how the number was reached.

📵

No Signal at the Job Site

Most split calculators are web apps that require a connection. When you're settling up at a job site in a signal dead zone, they're useless. SkyeSplitEngine runs fully offline — calculate and share the breakdown right there on the job, then sync the record when you're back in range.

📂

No Paper Trail

Splits calculated verbally or in a text thread create no usable record for accounting, tax, or dispute resolution. SkyeSplitEngine exports a formatted payout breakdown — CSV for your accounting system, PDF for the team — every time, so there's always a record of who got what and why.

🔁

Re-Building the Same Split Every Time

Teams that run the same split structure on every job — same parties, same percentages, same deductions — waste time recreating it from scratch each time. SkyeSplitEngine lets you save split templates and apply them to any new job total in two taps. Same structure, instant calculation.

Features

Every split type. Full audit trail. Fully offline.

SkyeSplitEngine handles revenue splits, commission structures, shared expenses, and payout documentation — all without requiring a network connection.

💸

Revenue Split Calculator

Enter the total job value, add parties with their percentage or flat-amount share, apply any deductions (materials, overhead, platform fees), and SkyeSplitEngine calculates each party's payout instantly. See percentage-based and dollar-value results simultaneously with the full deduction breakdown.

📈

Commission Engine

Configure tiered, flat, or hybrid commission structures for sales teams and independent reps. Set thresholds, rates per tier, and bonus triggers — SkyeSplitEngine applies the commission rules to any sale amount and outputs the exact commission owed. Handles accelerators and caps correctly.

🧾

Shared Expense Splitter

Split shared costs — materials, travel, tools, subcontractor fees — across any number of parties with equal split, percentage split, or custom allocation. Track who paid what upfront and output the net settlement amounts: who owes who, and exactly how much.

📋

Saved Split Templates

Save any split structure as a named template — parties, percentages, deduction categories — and apply it to any new revenue figure instantly. Standard splits for recurring crews, regular commission tiers for the sales team, or project-type splits by category. Build once, reuse always.

📤

CSV + PDF Export

Every split calculation exports to a formatted CSV (ready for QuickBooks, Wave, or any accounting system) and a clean PDF payout summary showing the total, each party, their percentage, deductions applied, and final take-home. The record that ends the argument before it starts.

🔄

Split History + Sync

Every split you run is saved to local history — searchable by job, date, party name, or total amount. History is stored offline-first in IndexedDB and synced to the cloud in the background when connected. Full audit trail without requiring a server to be available at calculation time.

👥

Party Profiles

Save recurring parties — team members, partners, subcontractors — with their standard split percentage, payment method preference, and contact info. When you start a new split, add saved parties in one tap instead of re-entering details. Profiles sync across devices through background sync.

📱

Offline-First PWA

SkyeSplitEngine installs on iOS and Android as a home screen app — no app store. The full engine, all saved templates, and all party profiles are available completely offline. Calculate splits in a dead zone, export the PDF on the spot, and sync the record when you're back in range.

How It Works

Enter Total → Add Parties → Apply Deductions → Export

01
Input

Enter the total revenue or bill amount for the job or transaction

Open SkyeSplitEngine and enter the gross amount — total revenue collected, invoice total, or bill to be split. Apply any top-line deductions (platform fees, material costs, taxes) before the split is calculated. The engine works from the net distributable amount, not the gross, ensuring everyone's split reflects the actual take.

02
Parties

Add parties — from saved profiles or manually — with their split percentage or flat amount

Add each party to the split — pull from saved profiles or enter manually. Assign each party a percentage share or a flat-amount take. The engine validates that all splits total 100% (for percentage mode) and flags any gap or overage in real-time. Supports up to 20 parties per split calculation.

03
Calculate

Review the full breakdown — payout per party, deductions applied, totals verified

The split result screen shows every party's name, percentage, gross share, deductions allocated to them, and net payout — all on one screen. Each number is traceable back to the inputs. Share the breakdown screen directly with parties via a screenshot or tap Export to generate the formal PDF summary.

04
Export

Export CSV for accounting, PDF for the team, and save the record to history

Export the calculation as a formatted CSV (party names, amounts, dates — ready for your accounting tool) or a clean PDF payout summary for team distribution. The split is automatically saved to your local history with a timestamp, job reference, and party names — searchable and synced to the cloud in the background.
