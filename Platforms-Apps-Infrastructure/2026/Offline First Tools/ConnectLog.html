The Problem

The best business connections fail because of poor follow-through, not poor chemistry

🗞️

Business Cards Get Lost

You collect a stack of cards at an event, promise to follow up, get back to your desk, and the stack sits until you throw it away. ConnectLog lets you log the connection right after the conversation — while you still remember the context.

💻

CRMs Are Overkill for Solo Operators

HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive — all the features you don't need, plus a subscription fee, cloud storage of your contact data, and a learning curve. ConnectLog is a fast, local contact log — no pipeline stages, no deal tracking, just people and conversations.

📶

Events Have No Wi-Fi When You Need It

Conference centers, trade show floors, and networking events all have spotty Wi-Fi. Cloud-based contact apps fail exactly when you need them most. ConnectLog is an offline-first PWA — it works at the event, syncs when you're back on network.

⏰

Follow-Ups Without Reminders Don't Happen

Good intentions at the event don't become follow-up emails a week later without a reminder system. ConnectLog lets you set a follow-up date per contact and surfaces due follow-ups prominently so you don't forget the people who matter.

Features

Log connections. Remember conversations. Follow through.

👤

Fast Contact Logging

Add a contact in under 30 seconds — name, company, how you met, tags, and your conversation notes. No required fields beyond a name. Log right after the conversation while the context is fresh, even if all you have is "Dave, blockchain startup, needs ops tools."

🏷️

Contact Type Tags

Tag every contact as Lead, Partner, Vendor, Collaborator, Referral, Mentor, or any custom tag you define. Filter your contact log by tag to see all your current leads, all your referral sources, or all your potential collaborators in one view.

⏰

Follow-Up Reminders

Set a follow-up date per contact and ConnectLog surfaces it on your dashboard when the date is due. Due follow-ups are shown with a subtle badge so you never open the app without knowing who needs your attention today.

🔍

Full-Text Search

Search across all contact names, company names, your conversation notes, and tags simultaneously. Find that developer you met at a Phoenix event two months ago with one keyword — no scrolling, no filtering required.

📶

Offline-First PWA

ConnectLog installs as a PWA and runs fully offline. Log contacts at events, conferences, and networking meetups without internet. Entries are stored locally and available immediately — no sync delay, no "you're offline" error at the worst moment.

🗓️

Interaction Timeline

Every contact has an interaction timeline — log each call, meeting, email, or LinkedIn message with a date and brief note. See your entire relationship history with a contact in chronological order at a glance before your next conversation.

📎

Contact Notes & Context

Store any context that helps you remember the person — what they're working on, what they need, what you offered, who referred them, topics they're interested in. Rich notes turn a name into a relationship you can actually act on.

💾

Export & Backup

Export your entire contact log as a JSON or CSV file for backup or migration. Import it back on any device. Your networking history is your asset — it stays in your hands, not on a vendor's server that decides your data terms.

How It Works

Install → Log → Tag → Follow Up

01
Install

Install ConnectLog as a PWA on your phone before your next event

Open ConnectLog on your phone and install it from the PWA prompt. It runs fully offline from that point — ready for the conference floor, the meetup, the hallway conversation, or the coffee meeting where you need to log a contact without hunting for Wi-Fi.

02
Log

Log the contact immediately after every meaningful conversation

While the conversation is fresh — right after, or on the way back to your seat — open ConnectLog and add the person with their name, company, how you met, and your notes about what you discussed. 30 seconds while the context is live beats 30 minutes of reconstruction later.

03
Tag

Tag each contact with type and set a follow-up date if action is needed

Tag the contact as Lead, Partner, Referral, or your custom types. If they need a follow-up email, call, or introduction, set a follow-up date. ConnectLog will surface the reminder on the due date — no manual checking of dates required.

04
Follow Up

Open ConnectLog's dashboard to see who needs attention today

The ConnectLog dashboard shows due follow-ups at the top, recently added contacts below, and your full searchable log below that. Start each work day by checking ConnectLog before your email — see who you owe a response and act before the connection goes cold.
