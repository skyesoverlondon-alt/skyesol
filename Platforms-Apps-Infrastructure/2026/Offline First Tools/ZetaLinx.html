The Problem

You have 20 important links and one place to put them — a bio field with 150 characters

🔗

Bio Links Can't Fit Your Ecosystem

One Instagram bio link. One Twitter/X profile URL. You have a booking link, a portfolio, a resource page, a shop, a consultation form, a newsletter signup, and five tools you've shipped. ZetaLinx gives you a single hub URL that links to all of them, organized by category.

💸

Linktree Charges for Features You Need

Custom branding, analytics, scheduling, multiple pages — these are Pro or higher Linktree features behind a monthly subscription. ZetaLinx gives you all of it as an offline-first app you control and operate yourself, with no vendor dependency.

🙅

No Way to Know What Links Get Clicked

If you're sending people to a list of links and you can't see which ones get clicked, you're guessing which content resonates. ZetaLinx tracks clicks per link so you know which of your offers, tools, and content people actually act on — and which ones to cut.

📅

Promotions Need a Different Link Set

When you run a launch, limited offer, or event, you want to temporarily surface specific links and hide others — without manually managing them. ZetaLinx's scheduled links let you set start/end dates so your link page automatically shows the right links at the right time.

Features

Every link you share. One hub you control.

🌐

Custom Link-in-Bio Page

Build a fully branded link page with your name, bio, avatar, custom colors, and unlimited link rows. Share one URL across every platform and profile — everyone lands on your hub and chooses where to go next. Your brand, your page, your rules.

📊

Click Tracking & Analytics

ZetaLinx counts every click per link and shows totals, trends, and top performers in your analytics panel. See which offers get attention, which content resonates, and which links you can cut — data-driven link management without a full analytics platform.

🗂️

Multi-Page Link Hubs

Create separate link pages for different audiences — a client hub, a social media hub, a per-project hub, or a division hub. Each page has its own URL, branding, and link set. Switch between managing all your pages from one ZetaLinx dashboard.

📅

Scheduled Links

Set a start and end date per link and it automatically appears and disappears from your public page. Run a launch with a "limited time offer" link, schedule your event registration link for exactly the open period, and never manually edit your page for time-based promotions again.

📶

Offline-First PWA

ZetaLinx runs offline-first. Manage your link library, edit your hub page, and view analytics without an internet connection. Install on your phone and desktop — your links and page settings are always available regardless of connectivity.

🏷️

Link Categories

Organize your links into named category sections on your hub page — "Book a Call", "Free Resources", "Tools I Built", "Connect". Visitors can scan by category to find what they need. Clean, organized hub pages convert better than a long unsorted list.

📤

QR Code Per Page

Generate a QR code for any ZetaLinx hub page and download it for print — business cards, event materials, presentations, signage. Scan leads directly to your full link hub. Update the links behind the QR code without reprinting the code.

💾

Export & Backup

Export your link library and hub page configuration as JSON for full backup and portability. Import it back on any device or migrate to a new deployment without losing your link history, click data, or page settings. You own your link data.

How It Works

Build → Organize → Share → Track

01
Build

Set up your hub page with your name, bio, and branding

Open ZetaLinx, enter your name, bio, avatar emoji or image, and pick your page's accent color. Then add your links one by one — label, URL, icon, category, and optional description. Your hub page previews in real time as you build.

02
Organize

Group links into categories and set any time-sensitive links to auto-schedule

Create category sections and drag links into them. For any link that should only appear during a specific window, set a start and end date. Reorder categories by dragging. Preview mobile and desktop views before publishing.

03
Share

Drop your ZetaLinx hub URL into every bio, profile, and signature

Copy your hub URL and paste it into your Instagram bio, Twitter/X profile, LinkedIn About section, email signature, and business card. Download the QR code for physical materials. Now every channel points to one organized hub you control.

04
Track

Watch your analytics panel to see which links get clicked and optimize

ZetaLinx records every click with a timestamp. Your analytics panel shows click counts per link, total hub traffic, and trend lines over time. Use click data to decide which links to promote at the top, which to cut, and which content to create more of.
