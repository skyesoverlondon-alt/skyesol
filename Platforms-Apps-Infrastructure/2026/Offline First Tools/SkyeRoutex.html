    What does “offline-first” actually mean?

    Offline-first means the workflow is designed to function even when network availability is poor. Instead of failing hard when the signal drops, the app keeps the core route and status flow usable and consistent.

    What’s the point of “exportable logs”?

    Exports give you a portable evidence trail: route activity, stop status, timestamps, and completion details. That helps with billing, internal review, and resolving disputes faster because the record is structured.

    Is Vault Gate required?

    No. Vault Gate is optional. Enable it when you want an extra access layer (PIN) for shared devices or sensitive ops. In the demo, you can simulate “remember unlock for 12 hours” like a shift window.

    Where do I start?

    Launch SkyeRouteX, create a route, add stops, then run your normal status flow. The goal is a clean day: consistent updates + a usable completion record.
