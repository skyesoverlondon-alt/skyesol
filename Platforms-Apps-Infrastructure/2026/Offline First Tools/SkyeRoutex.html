FAQ

Direct answers. No fluff.

What does “offline-first” mean here?

It means the dispatch workflow is designed to keep functioning when connectivity is poor. The system prioritizes continuity so operations don’t stall because signal is bad.

Why are exportable logs a big deal?

Exportable logs turn dispatch activity into a portable, reviewable record. That’s how you reconcile a day, resolve disputes faster, and move billing without chasing screenshots.

Is Vault Gate required?

No. Vault Gate is optional. Use it when you need access control on shared devices or sensitive dispatch ops.

What’s the fastest way to see it?

Open the app, unlock the console, create a new route, then use the dashboard flow to update status and add entries.
