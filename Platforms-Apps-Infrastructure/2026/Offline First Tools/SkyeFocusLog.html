The Solution
SkyeFocus — Work First, Log Second, Nothing Else
SkyeFocus is engineered around one principle: the tool disappears when you're working and surfaces when you need it. Timed focus blocks, structured session logging, and a local history vault — all running client-side, all available offline, zero accounts required.
⏱️

Configurable Focus Blocks

Set any duration — 25-minute Pomodoro, 50-minute deep work, 90-minute flow state blocks. The timer runs client-side. Close the tab, reopen it, go offline mid-session — the clock keeps going. No sync dependency on the countdown.

📓

Session Logging at Close

When a block ends, log what you shipped: task name, output summary, tags, and a quality rating. The log entry writes to IndexedDB in milliseconds. Your session record is permanent and local the moment you commit it.

📴

100% Offline Operation

Service workers cache the full app shell on first load. Every subsequent open — online or off — is instant. Running in a conference room, on a plane, in a client's building with no guest Wi-Fi: SkyeFocus doesn't care.

🔎

Local Session History & Search

Every closed session is stored and searchable. Filter by date range, task tag, or keyword. Results return client-side in real time — no API call, no loading state, no pagination limit. Your entire work history on device.

📊

Productivity Patterns — On Device

Daily and weekly session summaries. Total focused hours, block completion rate, most productive tags. Analytics computed locally from your IndexedDB log — no data leaves the device, no third-party analytics involved.

📤

Export Logs Anywhere

Export your session history as CSV, JSON, or Markdown. Drop it into SkyeDocx for a weekly deliverable report, SkyeSheets for analysis, or a client invoice as time verification. Portable output, zero vendor lock-in.

Workflow
A Focus Session in Five Moves
From open to logged output in under 30 seconds. No onboarding. No workspace setup. No account prompt.
01

Open or Install SkyeFocus

Go to offlineminiopssuite.netlify.app/skyefocus or install the PWA. First load caches the full app. Every open after — including fully offline — is instantaneous. No login screen, no account wall, straight to the timer.

02

Name Your Block and Start

Label the session (task or project name), set the duration, and start. That's two fields and one click. The timer begins immediately — no confirmation dialogs, no loading states, no tutorial overlay. Work starts now.

03

Work Through the Block

The app stays out of the way. No mid-session prompts. No notification spam. The countdown runs client-side — you can minimize the tab, switch apps, or go offline. The timer doesn't care about your network state.

04

Log Your Output When Done

Session ends. A simple log form surfaces: what did you ship, quality rating, tags. Fill it in while it's fresh — takes 15 seconds. Hit save and the entry writes to local IndexedDB instantly. That session is permanently recorded on device.

05

Review History and Export

Open the log view any time. Search, filter by date or tag, see daily totals and completion rates. Export the full log to CSV or JSON when you need to report output, invoice time, or archive a sprint's work record.

💻

Log every billable session with timestamp and output notes. When a client questions your hours, your local log is the receipt — timestamped, tagged, and exportable in seconds. No cloud platform required as evidence.

Track what you shipped each day across projects without a project management subscription. Daily session exports feed directly into status reports, sprint summaries, and stakeholder updates.
