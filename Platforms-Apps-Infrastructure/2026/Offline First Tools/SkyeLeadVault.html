The Problem

You're losing leads to friction you don't even see

You had the conversation. You got the card. You said "I'll follow up." Then the contact sat in a Notes app note, a Google Sheet nobody updates, or a business card on your desk — and the deal went cold.

📋

Leads Living in Notes Apps

A name and number in Apple Notes is not a pipeline. There's no stage, no follow-up log, no way to see who you haven't talked to in 10 days. Leads that go un-tracked go un-closed.

💸

CRM Tools You Won't Use

HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive — all built for teams with 12-step onboarding and a monthly bill. Solo operators and small shops don't need a CRM. They need a vault that works before the WiFi does.

📵

Offline Blind Spots

You're at a trade show, a job site, or an event — no signal, no cloud sync. Web-based CRMs go blank. SkyeLeadVault keeps working because your data lives on your device, not on someone else's server.

🔒

Your Leads in Someone Else's Database

Free cloud CRMs make you the product. Your client list, your pipeline, your contact data — stored on their servers, used by their analytics, at risk when their funding runs out. SkyeLeadVault stores nothing off-device.

Features

Everything a solo operator needs to track deals.

No bloated feature sets, no onboarding videos, no monthly seat fees. Just a fast, focused lead vault that works offline by default.

👤

Lead Capture Form

Add a new lead in under 20 seconds. Name, company, email, phone, lead source, and an optional quick note. Every field is optional except the name — get the basics down fast so you don't lose the contact while the conversation is still fresh.

📊

Four-Stage Pipeline

Every lead moves through four stages — New, Contacted, Qualified, and Closed. One tap to advance a stage. The pipeline view shows counts at each stage so you can see where your funnel is healthy and where it's stalling at a glance.

📝

Timestamped Notes Log

Log call notes, meeting takeaways, objections, and next steps against each lead. Every note is timestamped. You'll never open a deal and wonder "wait, where did I leave this?" — the log tells you exactly what happened and when.

🔍

Search & Filter

Search leads by name, company, or email. Filter the pipeline by stage to see only New leads, only Qualified prospects, or only Closed deals. Sort by date added or last activity. Find any contact instantly without scrolling.

📂

Export to CSV & JSON

Export your entire lead vault — or a filtered subset — to CSV or JSON. Drop the CSV into Google Sheets, import it into a CRM when you're ready to graduate, or hand it to a VA for follow-up. Your data, your format, any time.

📱

Installable PWA

Install SkyeLeadVault to your phone or desktop home screen like a native app. Open it without typing a URL. The service worker caches the full app so it loads instantly — even with no connection. One tap, full vault.

⚡

100% Offline — IndexedDB Storage

Your leads are stored in IndexedDB — the browser's built-in local database. Nothing leaves your device. No sync to a server, no account required, no login. Trade shows, job sites, dead zones — the vault works everywhere you do.

🔁

Import from CSV

Already have contacts in a spreadsheet? Import a CSV directly into SkyeLeadVault. Map columns to vault fields, preview the import, and load your existing list into the pipeline — no retyping, no copy-paste contact by contact.

🏷️

Lead Source Tagging

Tag every lead with how they found you — referral, trade show, cold outreach, website form, ad, walk-in, event. Over time, lead source tags show you exactly which channels are generating real pipeline and which are generating noise.

How It Works

Capture → Track → Close → Export

Four stages, zero friction — from the moment you meet a contact to the moment you close the deal.

01
Capture

Add the lead while the conversation is still warm

Open SkyeLeadVault from your home screen or browser tab — no login. Fill in what you know: name, company, phone or email, and how you met them. Tag the source. Add a quick first note if you have context. The lead is saved locally in under 30 seconds before you even walk away from the conversation.

02
Track

Advance stages and log notes with every touchpoint

Each time you call, email, or meet with a lead, open their vault card and log a timestamped note — what was discussed, what the objection was, what the agreed next step is. Move them from New to Contacted to Qualified as momentum builds. The notes log gives you the full relationship history at a glance before every call.

03
Close

Mark deals won, archive deals lost, know your conversion rate

When a lead converts, move them to Closed. When a deal dies, archive it with a reason note. The pipeline dashboard shows your total conversion rate — how many leads entered the vault vs. how many closed. Use that number to evaluate which lead sources and which approaches are working.

04
Export

Export to CSV to hand off, analyze, or import into a CRM

When you're ready to hand leads to a VA, analyze your pipeline in a spreadsheet, or import into a full CRM like HubSpot or Salesforce, hit Export. The CSV includes every field — name, company, contact info, stage, source tag, notes log, and timestamps. Your vault goes with you wherever your workflow needs it.
