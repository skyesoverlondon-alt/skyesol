The Problem

Field teams can't run on tools that require a connection at every step

Warehouses, basements, remote job sites, dead zones between towns — field operators lose operational continuity every time the signal drops on cloud-only tools.

📵

Signal Dead Zones

Most field operations tools stop working the moment the signal drops. SkyeOpsConsole caches everything locally — job list, task assignments, log history — so operators can keep working regardless of connectivity. Nothing is lost when the signal returns.

📋

Paper Logs Nobody Keeps

Field teams fall back to paper logs when digital tools break in the field — and those logs get lost, misread, or never make it to the office system. SkyeOpsConsole replaces paper with a fast mobile-first log that works offline and syncs automatically.

🕐

Manual End-of-Day Reporting

When job status isn't captured in the field in real-time, supervisors chase team members for end-of-day updates that are already hours stale. SkyeOpsConsole auto-compiles a daily ops summary from timestamped field logs — no manual reporting required at the end of a long shift.

📱

Enterprise Tools for a Solo Operator Budget

Field operations management software built for enterprise teams costs hundreds per month and takes weeks to configure. SkyeOpsConsole is built for small operators — fast setup, offline-first, installable on any device, and priced for a one-person operation running jobs in the field.

Features

Built for the field. Fast when offline. Synced when you're back.

SkyeOpsConsole is not a watered-down mobile version of a desktop tool — it's an ops dashboard designed specifically for how field operators actually work during a shift.

📋

Offline Job Board

The complete job board — open, active, on-hold, and completed jobs — is available in full offline mode. Create new jobs, update status, add notes, and log completion without any network connection. The board syncs the moment connectivity is restored.

📝

Field Activity Log

Log timestamped notes on any job from the field — arrival time, work performed, issues encountered, parts used, and completion notes. Logs are stored locally with precise timestamps and synced to the project record on reconnect. No more reconstructing the day from memory.

👥

Team Task Assignment

Assign tasks to team members directly from the console — set priority level (critical, high, normal), attach a job, add instructions, and set a due time. Assignees see the task the next time they open the console, whether online or off. Completion is logged automatically.

🔄

Background Sync

SkyeOpsConsole uses the Browser Background Sync API to queue all offline actions — new logs, status updates, job completions — and push them to the cloud automatically when the device reconnects, even if the app is closed. Field data never gets lost in transit.

📊

Auto Daily Ops Summary

At the end of each shift, SkyeOpsConsole compiles an automatic daily operations summary from all logged activity — jobs completed, hours logged per job, notes added, and any flagged issues. Send it to the office with one tap, or export as PDF for record-keeping.

📍

Job Location Tags

Tag each job with a saved location — address, site name, or GPS coordinates captured at check-in. Location tags appear on the job record, in daily summaries, and in sync data — so dispatchers always know which jobs happened where, even for jobs created offline.

📷

Photo Evidence Log

Attach photos to any job or log entry — before and after shots, defect documentation, parts identification. Photos are stored locally in offline mode and uploaded in the background on reconnect. Visual job documentation that doesn't require a signal at the job site.

📱

Installable PWA

SkyeOpsConsole installs directly on any smartphone or tablet — iOS or Android — from the browser. No app store approval, no MDM required. The app icon lives on the home screen and opens full-screen, exactly like a native app, with no browser chrome in the way.

How It Works

Install → Load Jobs → Work the Field → Sync

01
Setup

Install the PWA and load your job list before leaving the office

Add SkyeOpsConsole to your home screen from any browser — iOS or Android. Log in on WiFi and the app caches your full job board, team roster, and any open tasks locally. Once cached, the console is available in full offline mode for the entire shift, no signal required.

02
Field Work

Log jobs, capture notes, attach photos, and update status from the job site

At each job — signal or no signal — open SkyeOpsConsole and log your activity. Check in, record arrival time, add field notes, attach photos of work performed, flag any issues, and mark completion when done. Every action is stored locally with a precise timestamp and queued for sync.

03
Sync

Reconnect anywhere — all offline data syncs automatically in the background

The moment the device reconnects — at the office, at a client site with WiFi, or back in cell range — SkyeOpsConsole's background sync pushes all queued logs, status updates, photo attachments, and job completions to the server. No manual upload, no button to press. The data is just there.

04
Report

End-of-shift summary auto-compiled — review, send, and done

At the close of the shift, SkyeOpsConsole generates a daily ops summary from all logged activity — total jobs worked, time at each site, field notes, issues flagged, and tasks completed. Review it in the app, add any final comments, and send it to the office or team channel with one tap.
