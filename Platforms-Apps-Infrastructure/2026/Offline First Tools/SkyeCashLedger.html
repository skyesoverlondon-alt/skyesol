The Problem
Financial Tools That Create More Risk Than They Solve
Most small-operator cash tracking tools require bank account connections, personal financial data stored on third-party servers, and monthly fees just to see your own records. The tools designed to give you financial clarity are the ones creating financial exposure.
🏦

Forced Bank Sync

Plaid / Open Banking Tools

QuickBooks, Wave, Mint — they all want read-access to your bank. That access aggregates your transaction history on their servers, creates a breach surface for your financial data, and requires active maintenance every time credentials change or tokens expire.

Credential ExposureBreach SurfaceToken Rot
💸

Subscription Wall on Your Own Data

SaaS Accounting Tools

FreshBooks, QuickBooks, Xero — the moment your subscription lapses, your records are inaccessible. You're renting access to your own financial history. Miss a payment and you can't pull records for a tax audit or client dispute.

Paywall on RecordsVendor Lock-InAudit Risk
🐢

Complexity for Simple Problems

Enterprise Accounting Overhead

Operators who need to log cash in and cash out don't need a chart of accounts, a reconciliation workflow, and a payroll module. They need a ledger. The tool complexity scales to enterprise — the user just needed to track a $500 payment.

Over-EngineeredLearning CurveWrong Tool
The Solution
SkyeCash — A Ledger That Works Like a Ledger
SkyeCash strips the problem back to first principles. You have money coming in and money going out. You want to see your balance, categorize entries, and export a clean record. No bank sync. No OAuth tokens. No server. Local, instant, permanent.
💵

Income & Expense Logging

Log any transaction in seconds — amount, description, category, date, and notes. Income and expense entries update your running balance instantly in the browser. No round-trip to a server. The math is done client-side before you finish typing.

📴

Full Offline Operation

SkyeCash is cached by a service worker on first load. Open it at a job site, in a car, on a plane, or in any location without connectivity. Every feature — entry logging, balance tracking, search, export — runs identically with zero network access.

🗂️

Category Tagging & Filtering

Tag every entry with a category — invoices, payroll, materials, overhead, travel, tools. Filter the ledger by category to see what a single cost center actually costs. Client-side filtering returns results instantly without a database query.

🔎

Full-Text Local Search

Search across entry descriptions, amounts, dates, and tags. Results are computed from IndexedDB locally — no API, no latency. Find a specific payment from six months ago in under a second without touching a network stack.

📊

Running Balance & Period Summaries

See your current balance at all times. Pull period summaries — weekly, monthly, custom date range. Income vs. expense breakdowns by category, computed locally from your ledger. Financial clarity without a BI tool subscription.

📤

Export-Ready Records

Export your full ledger or filtered date ranges as CSV, JSON, or formatted Markdown. Hand to your accountant, drop into a tax prep folder, attach to a client audit response, or archive to SkyeDocx. Clean portable output every time.

Workflow
Log a Transaction in Four Moves
From open to committed ledger entry in under 20 seconds. No onboarding wizard. No account setup. No bank authorization flow.
01

Open or Install SkyeCash

Navigate to offlineminiopssuite.netlify.app/skyecash or install the PWA. The service worker caches everything on first load. Every subsequent open — including fully offline — is instant. No login screen, no OAuth, straight to the ledger.

02

Log the Entry

Select income or expense, enter the amount, add a description, pick a category, and optionally add a note. Four fields. Hit save. The entry writes to IndexedDB in milliseconds and your running balance updates on screen before you lift your finger.

03

Browse, Filter, and Search Your Ledger

Open the ledger view to see all entries in reverse-chronological order. Filter by category, date range, or transaction type. Search by keyword or amount. Every result is client-side — no loading state, no pagination limit, no API call.

04

Export When You Need Clean Records

Export any filtered view or your complete ledger as CSV, JSON, or Markdown. The export is generated locally from IndexedDB — no server request, no processing delay. Clean, structured financial records ready for your accountant, your audit response, or your own archive.

Who It's For
Built for Operators Who Handle Real Money
SkyeCash is not a hobby budgeting tool. It's a cash-flow tracking layer for operators running projects, billing clients, and paying people — where incomplete records have real financial and legal consequences.
🧾

Freelancers & Consultants

Log every invoice payment and every operational expense as it happens. End-of-year tax prep goes from a scramble to a one-click export. Your accountant gets a clean CSV instead of a shoebox of bank statements.

🏗️

Field & Trade Operators

Log material costs, subcontractor payments, and job deposits on-site with no signal. When you're back in range, the ledger is complete and accurate — not reconstructed from memory three days later.

🏢

Small Business Operators

Track operating cash flow without a $50/month accounting subscription. Know exactly what you've invoiced, what you've paid out, and what your net position is — without touching a bank dashboard or waiting for sync.

🤝

Event & Project Managers

Run a project-specific ledger for every engagement. Log vendor payments, client deposits, and expense reimbursements by project tag. Export a complete project P&L when the engagement closes — no accountant required.

⚖️

Dispute-Proof Recordkeeping

When a client disputes a payment or a contractor claims they weren't paid, your local ledger is the first line of evidence. Timestamped, categorized, and exportable in seconds. The record that matters is on your device.

🌐

International & Remote Operators

No bank API to authorize across borders. No OAuth token to expire in a foreign country. No sync to wait for on a slow connection. SkyeCash logs cash flow wherever you are, in any currency you specify.
