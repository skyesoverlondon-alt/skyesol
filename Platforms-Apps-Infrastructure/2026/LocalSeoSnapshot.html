Scoring Engine

Six weighted dimensions. One clear number.

The 0–100 score isn't a vibe check. Every dimension has a validated weight based on how strongly it correlates with local pack ranking.

NAP Completeness 15%

Name, address, and phone consistency. Missing or inconsistent NAP data confuses Google's local index and suppresses your ranking.

Category Precision 15%

Primary category alignment with your actual service type. Wrong or generic categories push you out of the right searches.

Review Strength 22%

Your review count and velocity benchmarked against your top three local competitors — the highest-weighted dimension in the engine.

Content Cadence 16%

Monthly frequency of Google Posts and photo uploads. Active profiles signal relevance and freshness to the local algorithm.

Conversion Readiness 16%

Website presence and HTTPS security. Profiles without a linked, secure website hemorrhage conversion clicks to competitors.

Trust Signals 16%

Average star rating and total review count as absolute trust indicators — not relative to competitors, but as baseline credibility thresholds.

How It Works

From business name to full audit in five steps

01

Enter Your Business Details

Business name, category (50+ types), city/state, website URL, service radius, review count, average rating, and monthly post/photo frequency. Takes about 90 seconds.

02

Add Three Competitor Review Counts

Pull the review counts from your top three Google Maps competitors. The engine uses these to calculate your review gap, weekly pace target, and 90-day plan.

03

Generate Your Score

Hit Generate. The six-dimension engine computes your 0–100 score instantly — no API calls, no waiting, no backend. Runs fully in your browser.

04

Review Fixes and Action Plan

Your Top 10 Fixes are ranked by impact weight multiplied by your current dimension gap — the highest-ROI items surface first. The 30-day plan turns them into week-by-week tasks.

05

Export Branded PDF

Download your full report as a branded PDF via jsPDF — score gauge, dimension breakdown, fixes, action plan, competitor targets — all formatted and ready to share.
