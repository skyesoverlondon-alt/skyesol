Welcome to kAIxu Vision

The ultimate cinematic storyboarder & script visualizer.

Frame Setup

Use the sidebar to manage your frames. Each frame represents a specific cinematic beat or movie still.

kAIxu Rendering

Type your scene description and hit 'Render Scene'. kAIxu will generate a cinematic high-fidelity keyframe visual.

Ghostwriting

Use the kAIxu Ghostwriter buttons to instantly expand on action lines or generate realistic dialogue beats.

PDF Export

When finished, go to 'View Storyboard' and hit 'Export PDF' to download your formatted cinematic presentation.
