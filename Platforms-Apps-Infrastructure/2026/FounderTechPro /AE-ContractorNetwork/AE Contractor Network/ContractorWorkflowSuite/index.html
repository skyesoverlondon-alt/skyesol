Workflow

Recommended Flow

  1. Start in ContractorNetwork and confirm the contractor submission, evidence, and review status.
  2. Move into the Verify Suite when the case needs the broader evidence, receipts, invoices, letter, and proof-gap command surface.
  3. Use Contractor Income Verification when the operator needs the focused finance lane for packet issuance and CSV output.
  4. Open AE Flow when the contractor case needs outreach, follow-up, or operator execution.
  5. Use Skye Platinum when the operator needs executive review, client-level context, or command escalation.
  6. Finish in SkyeDocxPro when the workflow needs a polished letter, packet, or deliverable artifact.
