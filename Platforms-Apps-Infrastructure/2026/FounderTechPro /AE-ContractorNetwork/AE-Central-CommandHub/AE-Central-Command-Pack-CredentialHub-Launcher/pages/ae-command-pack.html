Free field stack

AE FLOW + ConnectLog

ConnectLog is the fast-capture tool for everyday life. It is where the AE stores the person they just met before the lead disappears into memory. Context tags, contact details, private notes, and quick offline-friendly capture make it ideal for regular life, neighborhood runs, events, errands, vendor stops, and every other moment where a business conversation can happen unexpectedly.

AE FLOW is the working pipeline engine. Once a lead is real, AE FLOW handles qualification, visit tracking, account conversion, deal building, follow-up timing, handoff packs, and pipeline visibility. It is the place where a contact stops being “somebody I met” and becomes “somebody I am actively working toward revenue.”
