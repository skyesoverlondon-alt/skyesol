How to position this lane

This is the middle ground between cheap brochure work and the bigger authority-orbit sale. It is ideal when the client needs a serious main site first.

Signature Launch

Use for cleaner launches, first serious site rebuilds, and smaller buyers who still need premium delivery.

Authority Build

Use when the current site is limiting conversion and trust but the buyer is not ready for a $45K+ main-site push.

Executive Platform

Use when the founder or company needs a polished command surface with more depth, stronger delivery, and room to scale.
