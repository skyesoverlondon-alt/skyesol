What the client is buying

The client is not just buying aesthetics or setup. They are buying depth, structure, and a cleaner operating reality.

Core delivery

  • Stakeholder discovery, positioning audit, and audience/persona mapping.
  • Primary logo, variants, color system, typography stack, and spacing/grid tokens.
  • Versioned design token library across CSS, Figma, and optionally Tailwind.
  • Motion and interaction language for hover, focus, loading, reveal, and transitions.
  • Collateral templates, brand books, handoff assets, and recorded training.
