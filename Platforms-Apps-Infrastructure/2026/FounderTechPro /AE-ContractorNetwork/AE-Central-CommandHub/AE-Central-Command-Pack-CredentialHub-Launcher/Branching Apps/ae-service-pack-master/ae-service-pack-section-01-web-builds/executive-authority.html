How to explain this without sounding fake

Keep it plain. The Prime is the conversion site. The satellites are real supporting sites with topic lanes, content cadence, and contextual links back into the Prime. The goal is sustained authority, not spam.

Prime site

Main conversion surface: services, proof, quotes, booking, intake, offers, and pages that close.

Satellite sites

Real sites with topic or geo lanes that publish consistently and route authority into the Prime with contextual links.

When to pitch which tier

Use the orbit size to match the client’s ambition and territory.

EA-I

Strong first authority cluster for a company wanting real motion without going maximum scope.

EA-II

Better topic coverage, more backlink sources, and stronger regional or category push.

EA-III

Use when the buyer is competing in crowded categories and needs a larger footprint.

EA-IV

Use when the company wants the biggest orbit on the page: 8 total sites and 32 blogs/month.
