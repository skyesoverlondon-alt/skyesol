What the client is buying

The client is buying a custom intake engine, routing logic, compliance configuration, dashboards, training, and 12 months of support and tuning.

Core delivery

  • Branded intake portal with dynamic forms and validation.
  • Role-based access, notifications, and receipts.
  • System integrations and API endpoints for status updates.
  • Audit logs, compliance reporting, and routing dashboards.
  • Training, go-live support, quarterly reviews, and ongoing tuning.
