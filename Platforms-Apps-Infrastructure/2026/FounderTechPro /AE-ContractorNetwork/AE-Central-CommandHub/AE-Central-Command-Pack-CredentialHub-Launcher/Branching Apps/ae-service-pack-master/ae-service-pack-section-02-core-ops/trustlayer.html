What the client is buying

The client is buying a trust kit: status/incident hub, policy stack, routing governance, headers guidance, and launch/cutover protection.

Core delivery

  • Status + incident page with embeds and uptime logic.
  • Policy stack refresh and security statement set.
  • Headers checklist, deny-state messaging, and routing controls.
  • Trust hub, uptime logs, and ownership mapping.
  • QA battery, rollback plan, and launch cutover support.
