Best Fit

Organizations juggling email, SMS, push, and in-app with no orchestration layer and no reliable reporting.

What the client is buying

The client is not just buying aesthetics or setup. They are buying depth, structure, and a cleaner operating reality.

Core delivery

  • Communication architecture across channels and sequences.
  • Transactional templates plus automated drips and branching logic.
  • Preference-center UI and domain authentication for deliverability control.
  • Delivery dashboards, channel analytics, and reporting surfaces.
  • Launch hardening, training, hypercare, and ongoing health checks at higher tiers.
