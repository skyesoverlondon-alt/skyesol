Who to target

DataPilot fits buyers with real operational pressure, real data, or real compliance concerns.
Best Fit

Compliance-heavy teams

Healthcare, legal, finance, or regulated teams that need AI with policy, audit trails, and resilient behavior.

Best Fit

Ops-heavy service firms

Operators who want AI embedded into workflows, not sitting in a disconnected chat window.

Best Fit

Internal tool buyers

Teams that need role-aware workspaces, prompt control, and logs they can export when leadership asks questions.

What the client is buying

The client is buying a governed AI workspace: role-based controls, prompt versioning, logs, offline protection, dashboards, and a launch plan.

Core delivery

  • Governed AI/data workspace built for a specific use case.
  • PromptOps controls with versioning and rollback.
  • Session journaling, exports, and audit evidence.
  • Offline queue, retry logic, and deny-state UX.
  • Usage visibility, telemetry, and launch support.

How to explain it

“This is the lane for buyers who need AI that can actually be governed. They are paying for the controls, logs, resilience, and operational fit — not just model access.”
