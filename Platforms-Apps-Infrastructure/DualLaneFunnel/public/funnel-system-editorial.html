Why Funnels Fail: Mixed Intent Creates Drop‑Off

Job seekers want speed, clarity, and trust. Employers want proof, process, and control. When one page tries to satisfy both, you get friction — and friction becomes abandonment.

The leak usually shows up in three places: (1) unclear next steps, (2) long forms that ask for everything up front, and (3) no operational follow‑through after submission. A “pretty site” isn’t a system.

This build fixes that by splitting the journey into two lanes, then enforcing a clean intake and routing posture.

Operators don’t “get motivated.” They reduce friction until output becomes inevitable.
— Dual‑Lane Funnel System principle
