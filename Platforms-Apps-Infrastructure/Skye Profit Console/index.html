Core Modules

Six Modules. One Console.

Everything you need to run clean books — no spreadsheet juggling, no separate apps.

📊

Dashboard

Live snapshot of Revenue, Expenses, Net Profit, Net Margin, Unreconciled Deposits, and Audit Readiness score for the active month. Quick-action buttons to jump to any module.

💰

Revenue Log

Log every sale with client name, category, payment method, linked invoice number, and notes. Export to CSV for your accountant or auditor in one click.

📋

Expense Tracker

Track expenses by COGS vs OpEx — separating delivery costs from overhead so your margin story holds up under scrutiny. Vendor, category, method, all logged.

🧾

Invoice Manager

Create invoices with client, issue date, due date, and amount. Track status (draft → paid → overdue) and log payment references so deposits can be matched.

🏦

Bank Deposits

Every dollar that hits your account gets its own deposit record. Mark each as reconciled once matched to revenue. Unreconciled count surfaces on the dashboard immediately.

🔄

Reconciliation

Match bank deposits to revenue entries — the last step that closes your monthly books. One-click reconcile with an audit trail of what matched to what.

6-Month Intelligence

Know If Your Revenue Is Actually Consistent

Lenders and investors don't care about one great month — they want to see consistent, repeatable performance. SkyeProfitConsole automatically measures your revenue and profit volatility using coefficient of variation so you know exactly where you stand.

  • 📈
    6-Month Average Revenue & Profit
    Automatically compiled from your entered data, no formulas needed.
  • 📉
    Volatility Score
    Coefficient of variation (std dev / mean) — lower is more consistent, shown instantly.
  • 🚨
    Audit Red Flags Detector
    Flags months with revenue but no expenses, unmatched deposits, missing invoices, and more.

Offline-First Architecture

Your Data Stays With You

SkyeProfitConsole runs entirely in your browser. No server sends your financial data anywhere. Export a backup anytime, import it on any device. Works without internet.

📱

Works Offline

Data is stored locally using browser storage. Open it on a plane, in a dead zone, anywhere. No Wi-Fi needed to run your books.

📤

Export JSON Backup

One button exports your entire data set as a JSON file. Import it on any device or browser to pick up exactly where you left off.

📊

CSV for Accountants

Revenue, Expenses, Invoices, and Deposits each export to individual CSV files. Drop them into QuickBooks, Excel, or hand them directly to your CPA.
