Tutorial

Onboard SkyeLedger with these steps.

Step 1 · Save the key

Store your `KAIXU_VIRTUAL_KEY` via the hero panel so every summary and stream authenticates through kAIxuGateway13.

Step 2 · Generate summary

Click "Generate summary" to call kAIxu Gate v2. The response fills the summary panel and updates the ledger meta data.

Step 3 · Stream commentary

Press "Stream insight" to start kAIxu Gate v2 stream, watch `meta/delta/done` events fill the timeline, and monitor the budget cards/diagnostics.
